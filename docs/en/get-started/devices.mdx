GenieX runs across three Snapdragon hardware families. Each has a different interface and backend story.

## **Compute (Windows ARM64)**

Snapdragon X-series laptops and desktops — Copilot+ PCs.

**Supported chipsets**
- Snapdragon X Elite
- Snapdragon X2 Elite

**How to access**
- **Physical** — any Snapdragon X-series laptop or desktop.
- **Cloud** — open a `Snapdragon X Elite` or `Snapdragon X2 Elite` device on the [Qualcomm Developer Cloud — Compute page](https://qdc.qualcomm.com/devices/compute).

**Interfaces** — [CLI](/en/run/cli/quickstart), [Python SDK](/en/run/python/quickstart), [Local Server](/en/run/cli/local-server).

## **Mobile (Android)**

Snapdragon 8-series mobile platforms.

**Supported chipsets**
- Snapdragon 8 Elite (`SM8750`)
- Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 (`SM8850`)
- Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 6

**How to access**
- **Physical** — any Snapdragon 8 Elite-series Android phone with USB debugging enabled.
- **Cloud** — open a `Snapdragon 8 Elite` or `Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5` device on the [Qualcomm Developer Cloud — Mobile page](https://qdc.qualcomm.com/devices/mobile).

**Interfaces** — [Android SDK](/en/run/android/quickstart) (Kotlin, Maven Central).

## **IoT (Linux ARM64)**

Qualcomm IoT platforms for edge inference — smart cameras, robotics, industrial automation.

**Supported chipsets**
- Qualcomm QCS9075
- Qualcomm QCS9100
- Qualcomm QCS8275

**How to access**
- **Physical** — Qualcomm IoT development kits (e.g. Dragonwing RB3 Gen 2, QCS8550 HDK) running Linux ARM64.
- **Cloud** — open a `Qualcomm Dragonwing IQ-9075` or `Qualcomm Dragonwing IQ-9100 Beta` device on the [Qualcomm Developer Cloud — IoT page](https://qdc.qualcomm.com/devices/iot).

**Interfaces** — [Docker image](/en/run/cli/install) (Linux ARM64 with NPU acceleration).
